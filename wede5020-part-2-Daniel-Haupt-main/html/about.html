About Us

Haupt Computers was established in 1993 as a PC sales and repair company.
Since then we expanded our range of services to include maintenance contracts, network design, and implementation.
We also supply a wide range of hardware and software products.
We are an independent company and are not tied to any particular manufacturer. This means we can offer unbiased advice and recommend the best products for your needs.
We pride ourselves on our customer service and strive to build long-term relationships with our clients.
We believe in providing high-quality products and services at competitive prices.
Our team of experienced technicians are on hand to provide support and assistance whenever you need it.
We look forward to working with you and helping you achieve your business goals.
